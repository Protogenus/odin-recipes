Lasagna Recipe

A famous baked Italian dish

A baked Italian dish consisting of wide strips of pasta cooked and layered with meat or vegetables, cheese, and tomato sauce.Large, flat, rectangular strips of pasta. a baked dish consisting of layers of this pasta, cheese, tomato sauce, and usually meat.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Minced Onion
  • Garlic
  • 1 Can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 2 tbs Sugar
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1 Egg
  • 3/4 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  1. Boil water and cook pasta
  2. Brown your meat
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and meat to a bowl
  4. Mix your spices in to the mixture
  5. Put your meat paste into a shallow dish
  6. Cover meat paste with your lasagna noodles
  7. Bake at 375 for 1 hour
